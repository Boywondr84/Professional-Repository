Steve Gadd Fanpage

American drummer, percussionist, and session musician, Gadd is one of the best-known and highly regarded session and studio drummers in the industry.
He has performed with artists such as Paul Simon, B.B. King, Eric Clapton, Steely Dan and Chick Corea to name a few.
